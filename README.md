The labs provide hands-on experience with real-world attacks and defenses across the machine learning lifecycle, including model theft, evasion attacks in computer vision and NLP, data poisoning, and generative AI vulnerabilities. Through a sequence of progressively advanced assignments, they involve implementing attack methodologies such as prompt injection, adversarial examples like FGSM and PGD, and model extraction, while also evaluating their effectiveness and exploring corresponding defenses. The overall goal is to build a deep understanding of attack vectors, analyze security risks, and develop robust defense strategies by bridging theoretical concepts with practical experimentation in modern AI systems .
